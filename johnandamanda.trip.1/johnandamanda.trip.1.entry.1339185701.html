Charlotte, North Carolina, United States
Friday, June 8, 2012

After months of planning, it's finally time to leave. Right now, Amanda and I are killing a couple hours at the airport bar in Charlotte.

We are both really excited, it hasn't seemed real until we actually got to the airport. I've been flying around the world for years, so I'm a good bit less nervous than Amanda. This is her first time out of the country, and only her second ever plane trip. It's commendable that that she has committed to a trip like this on her first ever time overseas. She is a trooper, and I know we will have a great time.

The time change might be a little rough because we will land at 7am GMT in London, which will be like trying to wake up at 2am EST. It shouldn't be much of a problem for Amanda. After working night shift for 3 years, she's used to running on little to no sleep.

We really appreciate all the well wishes we have gotten, and we look forward to updating everyone along the way!
