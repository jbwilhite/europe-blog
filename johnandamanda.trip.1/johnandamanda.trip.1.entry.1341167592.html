The Quick
After a long day of frustrating train travel we were so excited to find the Quick in Brugge. It was the only thing open after 1800 and even had ice and WiFi. Even though we never eat fast food at home we definitely enjoyed our Quick burgers and fries.

Croquettes
At the campground restaurant in Amsterdam we were given some sort of fried appetizer. We weren't sure what they were at the time but of course we ate them anyway and really enjoyed them. Later we found out they were croquettes and that croquette sandwiches were a popular Dutch food. Thia was a sandwich from a bar in Amsterdam.
